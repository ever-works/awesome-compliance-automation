# Vanta Automated Compliance

## Overview
Vanta Automated Compliance is a compliance automation platform that continuously monitors systems and controls to help organizations achieve and maintain security and privacy certifications (such as SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR and others). It automates evidence collection and control monitoring to streamline audit readiness for startups, mid‑market companies, and enterprises.

- **Category:** Other
- **Tags:** soc2, automation, multi-framework
- **Vendor/Brand:** Vanta

## Features

### Compliance Automation
- Automates key workflows required to achieve and maintain security and privacy certifications.
- Supports multiple frameworks (e.g., SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR and additional frameworks).
- Centralizes compliance activities in a single platform.

### Continuous Monitoring
- Continuously monitors systems and controls relevant to compliance.
- Detects gaps or control failures as they occur, rather than relying solely on point‑in‑time checks.

### Evidence Collection
- Automates collection of evidence required for audits across connected systems and tools.
- Reduces manual effort in gathering screenshots, configurations, and other proof of control operation.

### Audit Readiness
- Prepares organizations for formal audits by organizing controls, evidence, and documentation.
- Helps streamline and shorten the audit process by keeping materials current.

### Multi‑Framework Management
- Manages multiple compliance frameworks from one platform.
- Reuses overlapping controls and evidence across different standards (e.g., SOC 2 and ISO 27001) where applicable.

### Integrations & Data Ingestion
- Connects to a wide range of tools and systems (via the broader Vanta platform, which integrates with 400+ tools) to automatically ingest compliance‑relevant data.
- Uses these integrations to keep tests, controls, and evidence up to date.

### Use Cases & Organization Sizes
- Designed for startups, mid‑market organizations, and enterprises.
- Applicable across industries that require security and regulatory compliance.
