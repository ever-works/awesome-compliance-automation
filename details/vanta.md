## Overview

Vanta is a cloud-based trust management and compliance automation platform that centralizes and automates workflows for multiple security, privacy, and risk frameworks. It connects to infrastructure, identity, and business systems to monitor security controls, collect evidence, and help organizations prepare for and maintain certifications such as SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, and other frameworks.

## Key Capabilities

- Unified AI-powered trust management platform for compliance, risk, and customer trust
- Automation of evidence collection across 35+ security, privacy, and related frameworks
- Continuous monitoring of controls instead of point-in-time assessments
- Integrated risk and vendor risk management
- AI-driven security questionnaire handling and customer-facing Trust Center
- Support for organizations from startup to enterprise scale

## Features

### Automated Compliance

- Automated evidence collection for 35+ frameworks (e.g., SOC 2, ISO 27001, HITRUST, and others)
- Centralized management of multiple compliance programs in a single platform
- Continuous monitoring of technical and organizational controls
- Real-time visibility into control status and compliance progress
- Workflow automation to stay compliant over time (not just for initial audits)
- Audit preparation and ongoing audit-readiness support

### Continuous GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance)

- Continuous controls monitoring beyond point-in-time assessments
- Real-time alerts on control issues and gaps
- Integrated risk management capabilities within the same platform
- Centralized view of risk and compliance posture

### Risk Management

- Central platform to manage organizational risk
- Identification and tracking of security and compliance risks
- Alignment of risks with controls and remediation workflows

### Vendor Risk Management

- AI-assisted vendor risk reviews
- Continuous monitoring of vendor risk and third-party security posture
- Support for vendor assessments to stay ahead of new threats

### Customer Trust Management

- Questionnaire Automation to process and respond to customer security questionnaires
- AI-powered assistance for security reviews and due diligence requests
- Trust Center to publish and share security and compliance information with customers and prospects

### Streamlined Audits

- Automated collection and organization of audit evidence
- Tools to get “audit-ready” for frameworks like SOC 2 and ISO 27001
- Support for recurring audits through ongoing monitoring and evidence updates

### Multi-Framework Support

- Support for 35+ leading frameworks across:
  - Information security (e.g., SOC 2, ISO 27001, HITRUST)
  - Data privacy (e.g., GDPR and related privacy standards)
  - AI governance and other emerging frameworks

### AI and Automation

- AI agents and automated workflows to handle manual compliance and risk tasks
- Automated identification of gaps in security, risk, and compliance programs
- AI-powered assistance for questionnaires, vendor reviews, and trust communications

### Solutions by Company Size

- **Startup**: Support for achieving initial compliance (e.g., first SOC 2) to unlock larger customers
- **Mid-market**: Scalable processes and continuous visibility across programs
- **Enterprise**: Tailored solutions for complex environments with enterprise-grade security and controls
