## Sprinto AI

**Overview**

Sprinto AI is a comprehensive AI-native Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) platform designed to streamline compliance processes for organizations at various stages of growth. It focuses on automating evidence collection, updating controls, and maintaining continuous audit readiness, thereby reducing the manual effort required for compliance management.

**Features**

- **Automated Evidence Collection:** Sprinto AI automates the process of collecting evidence for audits, ensuring that organizations are always prepared for compliance checks.
- **Continuous Compliance:** Keeps your organization continuously audit-ready by automatically updating controls and evidence.
- **200+ Frameworks:** Includes over 200 compliance frameworks such as SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR, enabling rapid compliance with various standards.
- **300+ Integrations:** Connects seamlessly with over 300 systems, including AWS, Google Workspace, Okta, and GitHub, to automatically map integration data into audit readiness.
- **Intelligent Agents:** Leverages AI to create intelligent agents that manage compliance at scale, unifying audits, vendors, and risks.
- **Lead Auditor Guidance:** Provides guidance from ISO-certified lead auditors to ensure successful audit outcomes.

**Target Market**

- **Enterprises:** Offers risk management solutions to handle complex compliance challenges and provide defensible proof of compliance.
- **Mid-Market:** Delivers continuous compliance solutions to prevent teams from being overwhelmed with evidence gathering.
- **Startups:** Facilitates first-time compliance efforts to prevent delays in business growth and product momentum.
