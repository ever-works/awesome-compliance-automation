# Qualifi Trust Center

**Category:** SOC 2  
**Tags:** SOC2, Privacy  
**Website:** https://trust.qualifi.hr

## Overview
Qualifi Trust Center provides a centralized, real-time view of Qualifi’s security and compliance posture. It is designed to make it faster and more transparent for customers, partners, and auditors to review Qualifi’s security controls, certifications, and policies.

## Features
- **Real-time security and compliance overview** – Central hub for up-to-date information on Qualifi’s security posture.
- **Security controls documentation** – Describes the technical and organizational measures Qualifi uses to protect data.
- **Compliance information** – Shares details on relevant certifications and audit standards (e.g., SOC 2 and privacy-related practices).
- **Policy repository** – Central access to Qualifi’s security, privacy, and related governance policies.
- **Diligence support** – Streamlines security questionnaires and audits by consolidating key security and compliance information.
- **Transparent security communication** – Demonstrates Qualifi’s ongoing commitment to security and privacy to customers and stakeholders.

## Compliance & Standards
- Focus on **SOC 2** security and compliance requirements.
- Emphasis on **privacy** practices and controls.
