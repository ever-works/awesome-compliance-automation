# Vanta HIPAA Compliance Automation

**Category:** HIPAA  
**Brand:** Vanta  
**Website:** https://www.vanta.com/products/hipaa

## Overview
Vanta HIPAA Compliance Automation is a module designed for organizations that access, process, or store protected health information (PHI). It helps automate HIPAA compliance activities by integrating with commonly used business tools, continuously monitoring controls, and collecting evidence needed to demonstrate adherence to U.S. healthcare regulatory requirements.

## Features

- **Automated evidence collection**  
  - Automates up to 85% of the evidence collection required to demonstrate HIPAA compliance.  
  - Continuously collects and centralizes compliance artifacts to support audits and attestations.

- **Integrations with business tools**  
  - Connects with commonly used business and technical systems to map and monitor HIPAA-related controls.  
  - Uses these integrations to reduce manual compliance work and keep data in sync.

- **Access reviews**  
  - Consolidates account and user access data across systems.  
  - Supports periodic access reviews to ensure only approved users can access sensitive data and company tools.  
  - Helps strengthen security posture by identifying and remediating unnecessary or risky access.

- **Inventory management**  
  - Provides a single place to view all relevant inventory items (e.g., devices, assets, systems).  
  - Helps monitor where sensitive data is stored and track assets over time.  
  - Supports securing PHI across the full asset inventory.

- **Policies builder and templates**  
  - Offers bestâ€‘practice policy templates aligned with HIPAA and related security requirements.  
  - Enables creation and customization of policies from scratch.  
  - Centralizes policy management to support consistent implementation across the organization.

- **Central HIPAA compliance workspace**  
  - Maintains a single source of truth for HIPAA-related activities and status.  
  - Surfaces instant security and compliance reports to show progress towards HIPAA requirements.

- **Security training and communication**  
  - Includes capabilities to manage or track security training related to HIPAA.  
  - Utilizes notifications (e.g., Slack) to keep teams informed about required actions, changes, or issues.

- **Risk mitigation for HIPAA violations**  
  - Provides structure and guidance to reduce the likelihood of HIPAA violations.  
  - Focuses on protecting highly sensitive health information to avoid financial penalties and reputational damage.
