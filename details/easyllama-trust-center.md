## Overview

EasyLlama Trust Center provides a consolidated view of EasyLlama’s security, privacy, and compliance posture, helping customers evaluate the company’s controls and certifications. EasyLlama was founded in 2019 and is headquartered in Covina, California, USA.

## Compliance & Certifications

- **SOC 2 Type II certified**
- **GDPR-compliant**

## Security & Privacy

EasyLlama protects its customers with a range of security and privacy controls (as outlined in its public Trust Center), supporting:

- Protection of customer data through documented security practices
- Privacy safeguards aligned with GDPR requirements
- Transparent communication of security and compliance status via a public Trust Center

## Features

- **Public Trust Center** consolidating:
  - Security practices
  - Compliance certifications
  - Policies and documentation relevant to security and privacy
- **Security posture transparency** for assessing SOC 2 and related compliance
- **Privacy and data protection controls** that support GDPR compliance
