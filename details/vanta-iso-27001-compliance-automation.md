# Vanta ISO 27001 Compliance Automation

**Category:** ISO 27001  
**Brand:** Vanta  
**Website:** https://www.vanta.com/products/iso-27001

## Overview
Vanta ISO 27001 Compliance Automation is a module within the Vanta trust management platform that helps organizations achieve and maintain ISO 27001 certification. It automates evidence collection, continuous security monitoring, and audit preparation so teams can build and manage an Information Security Management System (ISMS) with less manual work.

## Key Capabilities
- Automates ISO 27001 compliance workflows from initial setup through ongoing maintenance
- Provides continuous monitoring of systems and controls
- Centralizes ISMS documentation, risks, and audit activities
- Supports extension to related ISO standards and other frameworks (e.g., SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR)

- Ongoing monitoring of connected systems for compliance drift

- Automatic generation and updating of the ISO 27001 Statement of Applicability (SoA)

  - Specific automated tests
  - Supporting documents and artifacts

### Access Reviews & Requests
- Automated pulling of account and access data from connected systems
- Workflows for periodic user access reviews
- Tracking of new access requests to ensure approvals are documented
- Helps ensure only authorized users can access sensitive systems and tools

### Issue Management
- Central place to track ISO-related issues and non-conformities
- Ability to:
  - Manage and document ISO non-conformities
  - Link relevant controls and policies to each issue
  - Route exceptions and deviations for review and approval
  - Track remediation and closure of gaps

### Risk Management
- Workflows based on ISO 27005 for risk assessment and treatment
- Support for:
  - Identifying information security risks
  - Prioritizing and evaluating risk impact and likelihood
  - Defining and tracking mitigation plans
- Designed for continuous risk management to keep the ISMS aligned with ISO 27001 requirements

### Internal Audit Workflows
- Tools to support internal audits for ISO 27001 readiness, including:
  - Assigning internal auditors
  - Tracking audit reviews and activities
  - Centralizing audit findings and follow-up actions
- Helps maintain ongoing readiness for external certification or surveillance audits

### ISO Extensions
- Ability to extend an ISO 27001 program to related ISO standards:
  - ISO 27017 (cloud security)
  - ISO 27018 (protection of personal data in the cloud)
  - ISO 27701 (privacy information management)
- Reuses existing ISO 27001 work to minimize incremental effort for these extensions

### Cross-Framework Reuse
- Reuse ISO 27001 controls, evidence, and tests across:
  - SOC 2
  - HIPAA
  - GDPR
- Visibility into how much of each additional framework is already covered, helping plan expansion into other standards more efficiently
