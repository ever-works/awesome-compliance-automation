## Overview
Vanta SOC 2 Compliance Automation is a cloud-based compliance automation and trust management product focused on SOC 2. It automates evidence collection, helps scope SOC 2 reports, guides implementation of security controls, centralizes documentation, and connects organizations with auditors to streamline SOC 2 audits.

## Features
- **SOC 2-focused compliance automation**
  - Designed specifically to support SOC 2 preparation and ongoing compliance.
  - Helps scope SOC 2 reports and align to SOC 2 trust service criteria.

- **Automated evidence collection**
  - Continuously and automatically gathers technical and procedural evidence required for SOC 2.
  - Reduces manual collection of screenshots, logs, and configuration exports.

- **Security control implementation guidance**
  - Guides organizations through required SOC 2 controls.
  - Assists in mapping existing controls and identifying gaps.

- **Centralized documentation**
  - Stores policies, procedures, and SOC 2–related documentation in one place.
  - Maintains a structured record of compliance activities and artifacts.

- **Audit preparation and collaboration**
  - Organizes SOC 2 evidence and documentation for auditor review.
  - Connects organizations with auditors to streamline formal SOC 2 audits.

- **Trust and compliance management platform**
  - Part of Vanta’s broader trust management platform for managing multiple compliance initiatives.
  - Integrates with other Vanta modules such as risk management, third-party risk, questionnaire automation, and trust center (via the platform navigation).

- **Integrations and automation ecosystem**
  - Connects to many external tools via Vanta’s integration layer (the SOC 2 product is built on the same platform that integrates with 400+ tools).
  - Supports API-based workflows via the Vanta API (for custom integrations and automation).
