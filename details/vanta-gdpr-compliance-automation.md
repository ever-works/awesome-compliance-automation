# Vanta GDPR Compliance Automation

## Overview
Vanta GDPR Compliance Automation is a module within the Vanta platform that helps organizations comply with the EU and UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). It centralizes GDPR-related activities, automates evidence collection, and supports ongoing monitoring of privacy and security controls.

## Key Capabilities
- Supports organizations in meeting GDPR requirements for protecting EU and UK residents’ personal data.
- Provides a single platform to manage GDPR controls, documentation, and evidence.
- Automates key compliance tasks to reduce manual effort and legal research.
- Continuously updates controls to reflect evolving GDPR regulations and requirements.

## Features
- **Policies builder and templates**  
  - Create, manage, and implement compliance policies within the Vanta platform.  
  - Use best-practice templates or build fully custom policies to match your organization’s needs.

- **Inventory management**  
  - Centralized view of inventory items (e.g., systems, assets, tools) relevant to GDPR.  
  - Track where sensitive data is stored to help monitor and secure personal data.

- **Automated evidence collection**  
  - Collect and organize compliance evidence automatically for GDPR controls.  
  - Maintain a single source of truth for audits, internal reviews, and ongoing compliance.

- **Document management**  
  - Upload and store GDPR-related documents and policies in one place.

- **Instant security reports**  
  - Generate security and compliance reports that reflect current GDPR control status.

- **Security awareness training (SAT)**  
  - Built-in security awareness training content developed in partnership with Living Security.  
  - Helps satisfy standard training controls required across multiple security and privacy frameworks, including GDPR-related training needs.

- **Access management support**  
  - Grant and revoke resource access through the Vanta platform to align with GDPR access control requirements.

- **Notifications and alerting**  
  - Configure Slack notifications to keep teams informed of important security and compliance changes related to GDPR.

- **Scalability and continuous monitoring**  
  - Maintain GDPR compliance as the organization grows through ongoing monitoring of controls and assets.
