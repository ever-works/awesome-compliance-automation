## Overview

Drata is an AI-native governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) platform that automates security and privacy frameworks such as HIPAA, SOC 2, and ISO 27001. It provides workflow automation, continuous control monitoring, automated evidence collection, and centralized tracking of assets and personnel. The HIPAA-focused solution includes policy templates, integrated HIPAA-approved training, and real-time reporting to reduce the operational effort and cost of maintaining compliance while protecting PHI.

## Key Use Cases

- Achieving and maintaining HIPAA compliance
- Managing SOC 2, ISO 27001, and other security/compliance frameworks
- Automating recurring compliance workflows and audits
- Centralizing risk, governance, and trust-related data
- Monitoring controls and collecting evidence continuously
- Tracking assets and personnel for compliance purposes
